About Us


Hello reader!
Our major objective behind creating this website is to facilitate the science of discovering, identifying, describing, classifying, and naming plants by using their images.

We have created this Plant Identification system that classifies different species of plants, using digital image processing techniques and helps the user to identify the plant being searched.

Here, Image processing is done to enhance an existing image or to sift out important information from it. Its main importance lies in dramatically boosting the performance of our model.
